How to Use the Practice System

The practice area draws from the comprehensive problem database embedded within each lesson. Use module review when you wish to focus on specific topics after studying. Use difficulty practice to progressively build your skills or challenge yourself with advanced questions.

Each problem includes hints and detailed explanations to guide your learning. Your practice progress is tracked by session, helping you identify areas for continued improvement.
