Left-Right Pointers

Two Sum in Sorted Array

Left at 0, right at end. Compare arr[left] + arr[right] vs target. Adjust pointers accordingly.

Container With Most Water

Area = width * min(height[left], height[right]). Move pointer with smaller height inward.

Valid Palindrome

Compare s[left] and s[right], move inward. Skip non-alnum.

Slow-Fast Pointers (Floyds)

Cycle Detection

Slow moves 1, fast moves 2. If they ever meet, cycle exists.

Find Middle of Linked List

When fast reaches end, slow is at middle.

Find Start of Cycle

After meeting, reset slow to start. Move both at 1x. They meet at cycle start.
