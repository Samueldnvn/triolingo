Module 3.1 Summary
Determinants: 2x2 and 3x3

This module covers 2x2 determinants, 3x3 cofactor expansion, and key properties.

3x3 Determinants (Cofactor Expansion)
Formula
3x3 Determinant
Expansion
$$\det\begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{bmatrix} = a\begin{vmatrix}e & f \\ h & i\end{vmatrix} - b\begin{vmatrix}d & f \\ g & i\end{vmatrix} + c\begin{vmatrix}d & e \\ g & h\end{vmatrix}$$
Properties
Properties
  • $$\det(AB) = \det(A)\det(B)$$
  • $$\det(A^T) = \det(A)$$
  • $$\det(kA) = k^n\det(A)$$ for nxx
  • $$\det(A) = 0$$ iff A is singular
