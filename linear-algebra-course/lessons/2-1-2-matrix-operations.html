Matrix Addition and Transpose
Overview

Matrices can be added, transposed, and multiplied by scalars, similar to vectors but arranged in rows and columns.

Definition
Matrix Addition
Formula
$$A + B = [a_{ij} + b_{ij}]$$
Definition
Scalar Multiplication
Formula
$$kA = [k \cdot a_{ij}]$$
Practice Problems
Problem 1
If $$A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{bmatrix}$$, find $$A^T$$
Problem 2
Find $$2\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 \end{bmatrix}$$
