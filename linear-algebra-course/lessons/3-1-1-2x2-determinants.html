Overview
Introduction to Determinants

Determinants are scalars associated with square matrices. They test invertibility and have geometric interpretations as scaling factors.

Definition
2x2 Determinant
Formula
$$\det\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} = ad - bc$$
Example
Calculating Determinants
Example

$$\det\begin{bmatrix} 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} = (2)(4) - (3)(1) = 8 - 3 = 5$$

Example

$$\det\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 \end{bmatrix} = (1)(6) - (2)(3) = 6 - 6 = 0$$ (singular!)
