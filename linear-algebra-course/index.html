Welcome to Linear Algebra

This course provides a thorough introduction to linear algebra with practical examples and exercises.

Course Structure

This course is organized into 5 chapters, covering everything you need to master linear algebra:

Chapter 1: Foundations

Start with the basics of numbers, notation, and vectors. This chapter includes 2 modules and 9 lessons.

Chapter 2: Matrices & Systems

Learn matrix operations and solving linear systems. This chapter includes 2 modules and 9 lessons.

Chapter 3: Determinants

Understand determinants and their properties. This chapter includes 1 module with 4 lessons.

Chapter 4: Advanced Topics

Explore eigenvalues, linear transformations, and orthogonality. This chapter includes 3 modules and 12 lessons.

Chapter 5: Applications

Apply your knowledge to real-world problems including SVD. This chapter includes 1 module with 2 lessons.
