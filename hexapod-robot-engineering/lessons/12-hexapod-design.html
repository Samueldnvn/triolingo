Module Overview

Capstone module combining all prior knowledge. Focused on hexapod-specific design: leg kinematics, gait planning, static stability.

Leg Kinematics

Hexapod has 18 DOFs: 6 legs × 3 joints. Three-joint leg provides position control in x,y,z. Inverse kinematics: desired foot position → joint angles.

Tripod Gait

Groups (1,4,5) and (2,3,6) alternate. At any time, 3 legs on ground forming stable triangle. COM projection always within support polygon.
