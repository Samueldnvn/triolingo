Module Overview

Control theory enables hexapods to track trajectories. PID control is standard. Stability analysis ensures safe operation. Frequency-domain design provides insight.

PID Control

\(u = K_p e + K_i\int e dt + K_d de/dt.\) Proportional responds to error, integral eliminates steady-state, derivative adds damping.

Stability

All poles in left half-plane (LHP) = stable. Any pole in right half-plane (RHP) = unstable. Poles on imaginary axis = marginally stable.

Frequency Response

Bode plots: magnitude 20log|G(jw)| and phase vs frequency. Gain margin > 6 dB, phase margin > 30° typical.
