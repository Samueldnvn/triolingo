Module Overview

Material properties affect hexapod weight, strength, durability. Steel vs aluminum vs carbon fiber. Understanding stress-strain, yield strength, endurance limit ensures reliable operation.

Stress and Strain

Stress: \(\sigma = F/A\) (Pa). Strain: \(\varepsilon = \Delta L/L\) (dimensionless). Young's Modulus: \(E = \sigma/\varepsilon\) (stiffness).

Material Properties

Yield strength \(\sigma_y\): plastic deformation begins. Ultimate tensile \(\sigma_{UTS}\). Fracture \(\sigma_f\). Endurance limit \(\sigma_e\): infinite fatigue life.
