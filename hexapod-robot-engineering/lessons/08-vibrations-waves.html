Module Overview

Vibrations affect hexapod structural integrity. Understanding natural frequency and resonance ensures safe operation without destructive oscillations.

Free Vibration

Natural frequency: \(\omega_n = \sqrt{k/m}.\) Underdamped (\(\zeta < 1\)) oscillates: \(x(t) = e^{-\zeta\omega_nt}(C_1\cos\omega_dt + C_2\sin\omega_dt).\)

Forced Vibration

\(m\ddot{x} + c\dot{x} + kx = F_0\cos(\omega t).\) Resonance when \(\omega \approx \omega_n\) causes large amplitude.
