## 🚀 How to Use

### For You (Use Directly)

Tell OpenClaw what course you want:

```
"Create a complete course on [subject] with [style/preferences]"
```

**Example prompts:**
- "Build a comprehensive Python programming course with a modern tech theme"
- "Create a 10-module American History course with an academic theme"
- "Make a Biology course with a nature theme, focus on ecology"
- "Build a Creative Writing course with a playful, creative theme"
- "Create a Mathematics course with a professional, clean theme"

### Skill Does the Work:

1. **Researches** your subject thoroughly (web search, authoritative sources)
2. **Plans** course structure (8-10 modules, logical progression)
3. **Designs** theme based on your preferences
4. **Creates** all content (explanations, examples, formulas)
5. **Adds** 300-500+ practice problems with hints & explanations
6. **Includes** quizzes and final exam
7. **Tests** and delivers the complete course

## 💡 Usage Tips

### 1. Be Specific

Good:
- "Create an 8-module Python course for beginners with 400+ problems"
- "Build a comprehensive American History course from 1900-2000"
- "Make a Biology course focused on genetics and evolution"

Less specific (still works):
- "Create a course on Python"
- "Build me a history course"

### 2. Specify Style

Good:
- "...with a modern tech theme (blue/cyan colors)"
- "...with an academic theme (formal, navy/gray)"
- "...with a playful theme for kids (bright colors)"

Omits (will choose default):
- "...with a construction theme"

### 3. Set Scope

Good:
- "...comprehensive course covering all major topics"
- "...introductory course for absolute beginners"
- "...advanced course for practitioners with fundamentals"
